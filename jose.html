JOSEPHINE RESTAURANT

Gen E. Aguinaldo Maharlika West Tagaytay City | Tagaytay City






Josephine's Restaurant in Tagaytay serves Filipino food at its finest and served one of the well known and best dish in Tagaytay "Bulalo"! My first visit at Jospehine's was long time ago. My children were still small that time and were with my in laws. This is my mom first time at Josephine's so I wanted it to be a memorable dining experience for her. Jospehine is located at the top of the ridge and has a spectacular view of Taal Volcano. The restaurant has floor to ceiling Windows and you may also choose to dine al fresco. Offer breakfast, lunch and dinner buffet

ADMISSION: Breakfast buffet: Php 405+
Lunch buffet: Php 495+
Dinner buffet: Php 495+

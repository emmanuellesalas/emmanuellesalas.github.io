Since the beginning of time, Tagaytay has been the run-away place for those who would like to have a quick getaway vacation. With the approximate distance of 56 kilometers from the heart of Manila, this is surely a place where you should go. This is most especially true to those bakasyonista who are coming from hot and humid temperature. There is no reason to travel far and climb Baguio City if you can also experience the same cold temperature in the nearby Tagaytay. Aside from its well-known cold temperature, It also has a variety of adventures to offer that will surely make your quick getaway very worth it! In this website, we will be giving you the list of the must-see places once you land the cold City of Tagaytay!

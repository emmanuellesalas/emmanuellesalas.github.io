THE LAKE HOTEL

Km 58 Emilio Aguinaldo Highway Maharlika East, Tagaytay, Luzon 4120, Philippines






Situated on the Tagaytay Ridge, an hourâ€™s drive from the city, The Lake Hotel offers the luxury of a swimming pool, free Wi-Fi and massage services. It overlooks Taal Volcano. Tourist attractions such as the Picnic Grove, People's Park and Calaruega Church are a 20-minute drive from the hotel. PAGCOR Casino is only a 5-minute drive away. Air-conditioned rooms at Tagaytay Lake Hotel are equipped with private bathroom facilities, flat-screen cable TV, and an in-room safe. A minibar and tea/coffee maker are also available. The Lake Hotel Tagaytay provides a well-equipped gym and an indoor swimming pool. Guests can also enjoy massage and spa treatments during their stay at the hotel. There are two restaurants which serve local specialities and international dishes. Warm beverages can be enjoyed at Bag of Beans Coffee Shop.

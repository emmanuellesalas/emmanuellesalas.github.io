CHARITO Bag of Beans

115 Aguinaldo Highway, Mendez Crossing West, Tagaytay City, 4120





One of the exciting dining places we visited in Tagaytay was the Bag of Beans. They have several branches in the place but we visited their main branch at Aguinaldo Highway. With the eclectic decors and pieces found at the place, the Bag of Beans is a fest to the senses. They offer freshly baked cakes and pastries and their pies are a must try.
